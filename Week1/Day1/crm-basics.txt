# 🚀 Salesforce Summer Program – Day 1 CRM Basics

## What is CRM
CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management. It helps companies manage customer data, communication, sales, and support services efficiently.

---

## Why Companies Use Salesforce
Companies use Salesforce to:
- Manage customer relationships
- Track sales and opportunities
- Improve customer support
- Automate workflows
- Store business data in one platform

---

# Salesforce Concepts

## Account
An Account represents a company or organization.

Example:
- College
- Hospital
- Company

---

## Contact
A Contact represents a person associated with an account.

Example:
- Student
- Patient
- Employee

---

## Opportunity
An Opportunity represents a potential business deal or process.

Example:
- Admission Process
- Treatment Process
- Event Registration

---

# Business Flow

Lead → Contact → Opportunity → Customer

- Lead = Interested person
- Contact = Identified person
- Opportunity = Possible deal/process
- Customer = Successful conversion
