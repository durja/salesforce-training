# Salesforce Summer Program – Day 1 Notes

## What is Salesforce?
Salesforce is a cloud-based Customer Relationship Management (CRM) platform used by organizations to manage customers, sales, services, marketing, and business processes efficiently. 
It helps companies store and organize customer data in one centralized system.

---

## What is CRM?
CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management. It is a technology used to manage customer interactions, business communication, sales activities, and support services.

### Benefits of CRM
- Improves customer relationships
- Organizes customer information
- Increases business productivity
- Helps track sales and support activities

---

## Why Companies Use Salesforce
Companies use Salesforce because it helps:
- Manage customer information efficiently
- Automate sales and support processes
- Improve communication with customers
- Generate reports and dashboards
- Increase productivity and business growth

---

## Real Business Problems Solved Using Salesforce
Salesforce helps solve many business problems such as:
- Difficulty in managing customer data
- Poor communication between teams
- Manual workflow processes
- Sales tracking issues
- Customer support management

---

## Salesforce Interface Basics
The Salesforce interface contains:
- Home Page
- Navigation Bar
- Dashboard
- Reports
- Leads
- Accounts
- Contacts

It provides an easy-to-use environment for managing business activities.

---

## Leads, Contacts, and Accounts

### Leads
Leads are potential customers who may be interested in a company’s product or service.

### Contacts
Contacts are people associated with an account or organization.

### Accounts
Accounts represent companies, businesses, or customers stored in Salesforce.

---

## Basic Workflow Understanding
A basic Salesforce workflow includes:
1. Capturing customer details
2. Managing leads
3. Converting leads into customers
4. Tracking sales activities
5. Providing customer support

---

## What are Objects?
Objects in Salesforce are database tables used to store information.

Examples:
- Account Object
- Contact Object
- Student Object

Objects contain fields and records.

---

## What are Records?
Records are individual entries or rows stored inside an object.

Example:
A single student’s details stored in the Student Object is called a record.

---

## Understanding Salesforce Data Structure
Salesforce stores data using:
- Objects
- Fields
- Records
- Relationships

This structure helps organize and manage business data efficiently.

---

## How Salesforce Applications are Built
Salesforce applications are built using:
- Standard and Custom Objects
- Workflows
- Automation Tools
- Apex Programming
- Lightning Components

Developers use these tools to create customized business applications.
